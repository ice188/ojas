Professional Beauty Treatments in Winnipeg

At SPA OJAS, we specialize in refined, results-driven beauty services designed to enhance your natural features. Our experienced technicians deliver precise brow artistry, elegant lash treatments, meticulous nail care, and gentle waxing in a calm, hygienic spa environment.

Brow Shape & Tint

Our brow shaping and tinting service is customized to your facial structure and skin tone. We sculpt clean, balanced brows and apply professional-grade tint for enhanced definition and symmetry.

  • Custom brow mapping
  • Natural or bold tint options
  • Long-lasting, polished finish

Brow Lamination

Brow lamination creates fuller, lifted brows by realigning brow hairs into a smooth, uniform shape. Ideal for sparse, uneven, or unruly brows, this treatment delivers a groomed look that lasts for weeks.

Lash Lift

Our lash lift enhances your natural lashes by lifting and curling them from the root. The result is longer-looking, brighter lashes without extensions or daily curling.

  • Natural curl and lift
  • Low maintenance
  • Perfect for everyday elegance

Manicure & Pedicure

Enjoy professional manicure and pedicure services designed for both beauty and hygiene. We focus on nail health, cuticle care, and flawless finishing in a relaxing spa setting.

  • Classic and spa manicure
  • Pedicure with exfoliation and massage
  • Clean, sanitized tools for every client

Waxing Services

Our waxing services provide smooth, long-lasting results using gentle techniques suitable for sensitive skin. We prioritize comfort, cleanliness, and precision.

  • Facial waxing
  • Body waxing
  • Quick, effective, and hygienic
