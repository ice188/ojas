After your session at SPA OJAS, we will issue an official receipt containing all necessary information (Practitioner Name, Registration Number, and Treatment Details). You can then submit this receipt to your insurance company via their mobile app or website for reimbursement.

What should I expect during a Face Rejuvenation Massage?

This treatment involves rhythmic, lifting strokes that stimulate lymphatic drainage and blood flow. It is non-invasive and provides an immediate "lifted" look, making it a favorite for clients preparing for special events.

Is the Hot Stone massage painful?

Not at all. The stones are heated to a comfortable, therapeutic temperature. Your therapist will always check with you to ensure the heat level is perfect for your skin sensitivity.
