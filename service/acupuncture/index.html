Restore Balance · Support Healing · Renew Energy

SPA OJAS offers professional acupuncture services rooted in traditional Eastern medicine and modern therapeutic principles. Located at 60 St. Anne’s Rd in Winnipeg, our clinic provides a calm, supportive space for natural healing and long-term wellness.

Acupuncture works by stimulating specific points along the body’s energy pathways, helping to restore balance, reduce discomfort, and support the body’s natural healing ability.

Traditional Acupuncture Therapy

Our traditional acupuncture treatments are tailored to each individual. By focusing on the root cause rather than symptoms alone, acupuncture helps regulate the nervous system and improve overall body function.

  • Supports pain relief and muscle recovery
  • Helps reduce stress and tension
  • Improves circulation and energy flow

Acupuncture for Stress & Modern Lifestyle

Daily stress, prolonged sitting, and digital lifestyles often lead to chronic discomfort. Acupuncture is especially effective for addressing tension-related conditions, fatigue, and imbalance caused by modern living.

Many clients choose acupuncture as part of a regular wellness routine to maintain physical and mental resilience.

Integrated Acupuncture & Acupressure

In addition to acupuncture, our treatments may include acupressure techniques to enhance circulation and deepen relaxation. This combination supports both physical alignment and energetic balance.
